ABOUT US

With the aim of attracting and retaining the 'brightest and best', we have drawn together a group of highly skilled and experienced marketers, researchers and strategists. Our consulting team are qualified to post-graduate levels and several have been awarded the highest level of Australian industry accreditation. Our consulting team regularly present to Board and Executive team level. We can assist with communicating the results and developing and implementing strategies to assist clients build their business, through a variety of ways that result in tangible improvements. We have the in-house resources to undertake all aspects of research without outsourcing. In addition to the personnel below, we have a team of interviewing staff, all trained and accredited, some of whom have been with us since our incorporation. We specialise in research, strategy and implementation, so we design research with the ultimate strategy in mind. This is important right from initial project set-up through to delivery of the strategy. Our market research division offers: • new & existing products research • customer satisfaction & benchmarking • customer retention & loyalty measures • staff/employee research • business model evaluation & development
